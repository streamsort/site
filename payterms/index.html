What is the Refund Policy?

Refunds are NOT applicable unless stated in the contract sent upon the purchase of our services or products. We may give a refund if we find out a product or service is fauly. We follow the UK Legislation which states that we do not have to offer a refund on custom-made items and software unless it is faulty. UK Refund Legislation (Click Here) Do you believe you need a refund for your purchase? Head over to the payment support page streamsort.com/pay

Policy on Recurring Payments
As soon as you cancel a recurring payment, we will bill you until the end of the term, for example if it was a recurring payment monthly and we charge you on the 1st day of every month and you cancel on the 2nd, we will stop charging you and the contract will expire the day before the next billing was supposed to take place. If however you got a contract to last a year for example, we will keep charging you until the year ends unless you pay an early cancellation fee. Head over to the support page if you would like to cancel a recurring payment streamsort.com/pay
