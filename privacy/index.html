UK Data Protection Act 2018 Compliance

Compliance

Any data we have collected is in compliance with the UK Data Protection Act 2018. Any data is saved in Manchester, UK and will not be sold. They are not classified as protected data however you can still view all data by going to your profile. In that page you do have ways to get your data deleted. You can either request for data to be deleted (except core and products data) or delete your account deleting all of the data (except identifier number).
