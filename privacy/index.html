Privacy Policy
Note
Please note anything in this page only includes the core StreamSort items. Other subsidaries such as NotesBytes will have their own privacy policy on their own respective websites.
Data We Need
For a StreamSort Accounts the only personal information we require is an email address. We link you to a unique identifying number and a password which will be stored encrypted.
Other Data We Collect
For billing, we need to collect your name, surname, middlename(s), country and currency which can optionally be stored for your convenience. We also do collect your billing information such as PayPal or Credit/Debit card numbers. We however do NOT store any of these and only use it once to bill.
For purchased products which links to our servers, we will collect IP addresses for your safety when required. We will notify you about this before purchasing a product.
Compliance

Any data we have collected is in compliance with the UK Data Protection Act 2018. Any data is saved in Manchester, UK or London, UK and will not be sold. They are not classified as protected data however you can still view all data by going to your profile. In that page you do have ways to get your data deleted. You can either request for data to be deleted (except core and products data) or delete your account deleting all of the data (except identifier number).

Billing Information (card information not saved) to Stripe - they are our card processors
