Unrecognised Payments

Hello! If you do not recognise a payment, let's see. If you have purchased anything from any companies listed in the main page for StreamSort at streamsort.com, we may use the "StreamSort" or "StreamSort.com" some transactions, however we will include the invoice number in additional information where we can. If you genuinely believe you are a victim of fraud, please contact fraud@streamsort.com with an email containing the full transaction information (e.g. Name, Transaction Date, Date Posted, Transaction info (e.g. Invoice numbers provided), last 4 digits of the card used). We will see what we can do and if not, we will ask you to contact your bank or building society or credit card company.

Where is my refund?

If we have agreed on a refund, it will be issued on our side immediately however will take a few days to process and clear. If it has been over 14 days, please contact support@streamsort.com

Requesting a refund

Most products do not include refunds unless specified in the page you buy it. This is due to it being digital downloads. If there however is a valid reason to get a refund, please email support@streamsort.com
