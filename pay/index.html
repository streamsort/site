MacroAIS Payments Support

I don't recognise a payment

Hello! If you do not recognise a payment, let's see. If you have purchased anything from any companies listed in the main page for MacroAIS Groups macroais.com we use the "MacroAIS" or "MacroAIS.com" name on all transactions, however the extra information may say which service you've used or invoice number or the actual statement may say "MacroAIS*NB" (indicating NotesBytes) for example. If you still have no idea what has happened, you may be a victim of fraud. Please contact us first at admin@macroais.com with the email containing the full transaction information (e.g. Name, Transaction Date, Date Posted, Transaction info (e.g. Invoice numbers provided), last 4 digits of the card used). We will see what we can do and if not, we will ask you to contact your bank or building society or credit card company.

Where is my refund?

Hello! If you have requested for a refund and we have approved it, the refund usually takes a few days to process so do not worry, it will not come immediately but will come eventually.

I want a refund

Our products and service do not include refunds unless specified in the buying page. If it is a custom item, please email info@macroais.com first. Please note that any disputes raised will lead us showing proof of lack of contact and there is no refund under our policy (unless the product/service you chose has a refund time)
